About

Hello, I am Dustin. I am a web developer and this is one of my practice projects. I built this site with SublimeText, Node.JS (server), and Bootstrap.

The idea here was to do a purely JS (vanilla) build of a simple to-do list. Started as a follow along refresher with watchandcode.com. Then once I finished the course I tried experiemnting with it and did my own thing. There is a lot more that could be done with this.

All the images were taken from unsplash.com, except of course for my picture.
